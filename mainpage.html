Tampa History Timeline

Tampa’s earliest history begins with the Tocobaga and Calusa tribes who lived around the bay.
The U.S. Army established Fort Brooke, marking the beginning of modern Tampa’s development.
Ybor City became the cigar capital of the world, attracting immigrants from Cuba, Spain, and Italy.
Tampa expanded rapidly with new neighborhoods, streetcars, and cultural growth.
Tampa is now a major hub for culture, sports, technology, and tourism.
